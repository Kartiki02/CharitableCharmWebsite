PROGRAMS

We have several projects running across India.

World Central Kitchen

World Central Kitchen (WCK) has served over 25 million meals to the hungry since 2010, and now it’s tackling emergency food relief during a global pandemic.

Crisis Text Line

When people need to talk to a crisis counselor ASAP, Crisis Text Line offers free 24/7 support.

Heart to Heart International

Heart to Heart International has shipped medical supplies to over 131 countries and given over $2 billion in aid to the less fortunate.

New York Times Neediest Cases Fund

It has been helping those in need since its inception in 1911. When COVID-19 swept the globe, the Neediest Cases Fund launched a special fundraiser for coronavirus relief.

Relief International

This nonprofit works in 16 different countries to provide food, education, and medical care to “people in the world’s most fragile settings.”

Cancer Research Institute

It funds research on immunotherapy treatment for all types of cancer. Its programs include training for emerging cancer researchers.

Hope for the Warriors

Hope for the Warriors provides what it calls a full cycle of care to service members, veterans, and their families.

Center for Reproductive Rights

This nonprofit envisions a world where every woman is treated as an equal and is free to make crucial decisions related to her own reproductive health.

The Alzheimer’s Association

The Alzheimer’s Association aims to fight and cure Alzheimer’s Disease from all sides. The organization also advocates for Alzheimer’s research nationally and at the state level.
